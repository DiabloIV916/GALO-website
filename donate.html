Your Impact

Every Gift Creates Opportunity

Your generosity helps remove barriers so more children and youth can participate in sports, receive academic support, and grow through mentorship. Every gift—large or small—helps young people learn, lead, and thrive.

$25

Support a Young Person

Helps provide sports equipment, learning materials, healthy snacks, and other program essentials that make participation possible.

$50

Create New Opportunities

Helps reduce financial barriers so more children can join inclusive sports, tutoring, and mentorship programs.

$100

Build Confidence

Supports meaningful experiences where youth develop confidence, teamwork, leadership, and lifelong friendships.

$250+

Strengthen Our Community

Helps expand programs, train volunteers, strengthen community partnerships, and create more opportunities for youth across Ontario.

Every Gift Matters

Whether you give once or choose to give regularly, your generosity helps children and youth discover their potential, build meaningful connections, and create brighter futures.
