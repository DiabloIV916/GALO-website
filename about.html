About Growth and Active Learning of Ontario (GALO)

Learn, Lead, Thrive.

Our Mission

Learn, Lead, Thrive.

To empower youth of all backgrounds and abilities by blending inclusive sports, academic tutoring, and peer mentoring. We build deep community connections across Ontario to raise the next generation of empathetic young leaders.

Our Vision

Belong, dream, achieve.

A vibrant, barrier-free community where every young person has the opportunity to play, learn, and lead with confidence.

V What we stand for
Our values

Connect, Include, Empower, Grow

🤝

Deep Connection

Bringing youth mentors, families, schools, and local organizations together into one strong, supportive community network.

🌈

Universal Inclusion

Creating safe, welcoming spaces where every single participant—regardless of their background or starting point—experiences a profound sense of belonging.

📢

Youth-Led Action

Giving young people real ownership to run front-line programs and drive authentic community impact.

🌱

Holistic Growth

Balancing physical fitness and academic support to nurture the total well-being and personal development of every youth
