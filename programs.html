Our Programs

We offer a variety of youth programs and community events

focused on sports, academics, creativity and leadership.

Discover what we offer and sign up!

Sports & Physical Health

Badminton Drop-In (Ages 6-18)

A fun, casual space for youth to practice their badminton skills, play friendly matches and stay active.

Basketball Drop-In (Ages 6-18)

Open gym time for youth to shoot hoops, play pickup games, and build community through sports.

Volleyball Drop-In (Ages 6-18)

A welcoming, energetic program to learn volleyball basics, practice sets, and play casual matches.

Academic Support

Tutoring Program (Ages 6-18)

Free homework help and academic support across various school subjects, guided by peer mentors.

Math Program (Ages 8–12)

Targeted math support designed to build confidence, solve tricky problems, and strengthen core math skills.

STEM Program (Ages 10–14)

Hands-on activities and fun experiments exploring Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math.

French Program (Ages 6–18)

Language practice sessions focusing on conversational skills, schoolwork help, and learning French vocabulary.

Register now
Leadership & Growth

Mentorship (Ages 12–18)

Peer-to-peer guidance matching youth with supportive mentors to build confidence and navigate life and school challenges.

Leadership Program (Ages 14–18)

Interactive workshops that teach essential teamwork, communication, and decision-making skills to build tomorrow's leaders.

Volunteer Program (Ages 14+)

An opportunity for high school and university students to gain experience, give back, and earn community hours.

Register now
Special Events & Family Nights

Family Fun Time (All Ages)

Community gatherings designed to bring families together for interactive games, activities, and bonding.

Pumpkin Carving Competition (All Ages)

A fun, creative autumn event where families team up to carve pumpkins, show off their artistic designs, and win prizes.

New Year Bingo Night (All Ages)

An energetic holiday event to celebrate the new year with friendly rounds of Bingo, snacks, and community raffles.
