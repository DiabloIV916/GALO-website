About us

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to be active, learn, grow, and feel like they belong..

GALO, which stands for Growth and Active Living Organization of Ontario, reflects its mission of empowering children and youth across Ontario through movement, mentorship, and personal growth.

The organization promotes both mental and physical well-being by offering sports, recreational activities, and active living programs designed for children and youth of all abilities. Guided by the belief that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of age or ability, GALO is committed to creating inclusive experiences that help young people build confidence, develop healthy habits, and reach their full potential.
